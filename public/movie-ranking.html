The Best Movies/ Series According to Pranab

My Top 3 movies of all-time.

Breaking Bad

A chemistry teacher starts cooking meth to provide for his family after being diagnosed with cancer, leading to a thrilling exploration of morality and consequences.

Better Call Saul

The prequel to Breaking Bad follows the story of a struggling lawyer who becomes the notorious Saul Goodman, delving into the backstory of beloved characters and introducing new ones.

Death Note

A high school student discovers a notebook that can kill anyone whose name is written in it, leading to a dangerous game of cat-and-mouse with a mysterious detective.
